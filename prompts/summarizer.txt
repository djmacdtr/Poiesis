{content}

{{

    progression of the chapter",
  "key_events": [
    "Concise description of each major plot event that occurred"
  ],
  "characters_featured": [
    "Name of each character who appeared or was significantly mentioned"

- 只返回合法 JSON，不要返回 markdown，不要附加说明。
